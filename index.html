Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior Portuguese knowledge to use this book?

No! Volume 1 is designed for complete beginners (A1 level). We start from the basics and build up progressively. If you're just starting your Portuguese journey, this is the perfect place to begin.

What's the difference between the printed and digital versions?

The content is identical. The printed version is a physical book you can write in and take anywhere. The digital version (e-book) can be accessed on your phone, tablet, or computer for immediate access. Choose based on your learning preference!

Can I use this book for self-study without a teacher?

Absolutely! The book includes a complete answer key so you can check your work and learn independently. It's designed for self-learners, though it also works great as supplementary material for classroom use.

How long does it take to complete the book?

The book is designed to be completed in 10 weeks at a pace of one exercise per day. However, you can go faster or slower based on your schedule and learning pace. The important thing is consistent practice!

Will this book help me speak Portuguese, or just write it?

The exercises are designed to build the foundation you need for speaking. You'll practice grammar patterns and vocabulary that you'll use in real conversations. While the book is written, the skills you develop are directly applicable to speaking Portuguese naturally.

What happens after I finish Volume 1?

After completing Volume 1, you'll be ready for Volume 2 (A2 level), which covers past tenses, future tense, more complex grammar, and expanded vocabulary. It's the perfect next step in your learning journey!
